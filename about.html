How to style sofas and armchairs:
The right sofas and chairs will transform your living space for the better—and the perfect accessories can add to this! Whether you've opted for a sofa and armchair in a stunning deep blue or a versatile neutral tone, beautiful scatter cushions in patterned fabrics will tie a room together and provide even more comfort when you're sitting down to read a book. To complete the look, our advice is to pair this all with the perfect textured rug and a stylish coffee table.

Need more inspiration?
Don't forget to check out our design inspiration guides for living rooms. Discover ideas for creating your dream space— whether you’re aiming for a cozy country style or a modern minimalist interior. You're bound to find helpful inspiration here.

Caring for your new sofas and armchairs:
Sofas and armchairs are an important purchase—they're going to be enjoyed by friends and family alike, time and time again. Once you've taken the time to carefully choose your favorites, it's essential to learn how to protect and maintain them to keep them looking beautiful.
